FAQ

Do classes change?
Yes! We usually have classes that reflect the current season and relevant sports, however certain classes are staples such as kick-boxing. Feel free to ask us about upcoming classes!
Are there on-site facilities?
On-site and available at all times is our state of the art locker-room, shower, and sauna. Other facilities such as the cafe, pool, track, and basketball gym are available but with their own set hours.
What if I can't make it to class?
No worries! Shoot us a message up to 24 hours before and you can reschedule or refund at your disclosure. After 24 hours we can only allow reschedules. Once class has started, we can no longer allow a recoup of costs.
Can I bring a guest?
For any of our open mat or open gym sessions, you're welcome to bring a guest! Simply check in at the front desk and register them to your name and you'll be all set.
